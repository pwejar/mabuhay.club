About us

Mabuhay Islander Bar & Grill, Spa, Boat and Yacht Pier and Eco-lodge is the perfect family holiday chill spot, located within the scenic Kilifi Creek. We are located in Mandharini, along Majajani road, which is 7kms from the Kilifi-Malindi Highway. We have a water front beach bar, restaurant and lounge offering the most unique Asian-western fused cuisines. Our signature meal is the Mongolian Hot Pot whose base soup is basically prepared for 7 hours and served in a special cooking pot where clients choose what to cook and eat from a variety of exotic meat and vegetables. We also have an exclusive Spa with a Jacuzzi and different massage treatments by masseuses from Thailand. Our boat yard and yacht pier provide parking, servicing and repair services of boats. We offer different water sports such as Jet skiing and Kite surfing. Our eco-lodge is located on an island with the most amazing sunset view. Some of the activities available include: birdwatching, camping, kayaking, movies under the stars, romantic bonfire dinners and ocean swimming. We also offer bookings for meetings, events, weddings, birthdays at very affordable rates. Mabuhay is the place to be if you want to experience an unforgettable mind-blowing experience.
